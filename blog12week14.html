WireShark

Posted by Abdul Rizwan on April 30, 2020

What is WireShark

WireShark is a open source network analysis tool which let you capture packets in real time and display them in human readable fromats. It has features like filters, color and many more which let you go deep into network traffic.

How to Capture Packets

There are many options to cature packets for exampe, if you want to capture traffic on your wireless network, click your wireless interface. You can configure advanced features by clicking Capture > Options, but this isnâ€™t necessary for now. After clicking interface name, you will see the packets start to appear in real time. Wireshark captures each packet sent to or from your system. By clicking Stop button it will stop capturing traffic.

Filters

If you want to capture traffic by filter you can apply that filter by typing it into the filter box at the top of the window and clicking apply. It will show you only the filter packets.

You can also click Analyze > Display Filters to choose a filter from among the default filters included in Wireshark. From here, you can add your own custom filters and save them to easily access them in the future.

Inspecting Packets

Lets say you want to inspect a packet all you need to do click on the packet you want to inspect and it will show you the its detail. On this you can also creat filters by right clicking on the detail.

WireShark is a very powerful tool and people use this tool to debug network protocol and examine security.
